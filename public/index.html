CoVRMmand

We use Google and require permissions to get your location from Latitude, you'll need to authenticate here.

Here's what'll happen:

Once you authorize a user is created for you on our database. This is is your email, a couple of tokens from Google, and your location, but only:

  • Once you check 'active' (and 'Update User').
    Then I will start asking Google for your location every ten minutes, updating the database, and displaying it to everyone on the map.
  • Once you un-check active (and 'Update User') I will stop getting your location and it won't display any more, however your last known location will remain in the database (I'm going to make it clear that out, I just need to write that bit).

For the curious

It's written with Ruby on Rails, and you can find all the code here, feel free to fork and hack away.

The site itself is running on a single Heroku dyno, so don't expect it to be too snappy.
